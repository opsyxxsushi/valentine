TO BE HONEST

Honestly, I like the way you talk, and I love how you laugh when we VC. It’s the kind of laugh that happens without thinking — real, effortless. I like how you don’t come with bad stories or rumors like other girls. There are a lot of things I never said out loud, but the truth is I feel calm and genuinely happy when I’m with you. I don’t talk to anyone the way I talk to you. My friends told me to give up on you, but I never did. I mean… why would I? To be honest, I never want to give up on you, even when I know I might not have a chance. There’s nothing to lose, right? I was never mad when you left me on delivered — not even a little. At the end of the day, you always replied, and that was enough for me. Sometimes I get jealous of Bryce and Ricky. I wonder how they got you. But when I really think about it, they’re probably a hundred times better than me. Still, I like how you’re always friendly, how you somehow end up by my side. And honestly… just tell me what you want. I’ll be that for you.
